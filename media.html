Take a look before you start

If you want to watch or hear how Dungeons and Dragons is played, take a look at a few of the podcasts and videos below.

Dungeons and Daddies

In this podcast, a group of improv actors and story writers come together to play Dungeons and Dragons for the first time. They play a group of dads who end up in the Forgotten Realms on the way to their kids' soccer game. The group quickly discovers therir kids have gone missing and they need to find them and find their way back to their own world!

Dimension 20: Fantasy High

Join yet again another group of improv actors who are all gethering to play Dungeons and Dragons for the first time. This time they are all in high school, except the school specializes in monster hunting. Join the group as they learn to play and figure out the mystery of who is casuing things in this world to start becoming amiss.
